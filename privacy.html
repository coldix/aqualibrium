Privacy Policy

Aqualibrium Ocean Adventures

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information when you visit `aqualibrium.oze.au` or make a booking enquiry.

1. Information Collection

When you submit a booking request or contact form, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and any details you provide about your enquiry (such as date, tour preference, and group size). This information is solely used to process your request and to communicate with you regarding your ocean experiences.

2. Use of Information

Your contact details are used exclusively to manage bookings, confirm availability, and ensure safe communication related to our tours and retreats. We do not use your information for unrelated marketing campaigns without explicit permission. Your data is not sold or rented to third parties.

3. Analytics and Cookies

Our site may use basic analytics to monitor site traffic and technical performance of the test domain. Cookies may be used minimally to store local preferences such as Light/Dark theme settings.

4. Security and Storage

We work to keep all submitted forms and enquiry details secure. Since this is a simple brochure and enquiry site, no direct financial transactions or credit card processing takes place directly through the website.

5. Third-Party Links

Our website may include links to external services such as Google Maps for our location. Please note that we do not govern the privacy practices of these external sites.

6. Contact Us
