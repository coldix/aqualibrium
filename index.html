About Aqualibrium

Aqualibrium creates small group ocean experiences built around timing, local knowledge and genuine connection to place.

From Fremantle, guests can kayak, snorkel and explore by e-bike through carefully chosen coastal environments, with each session shaped around the best available conditions.

The wider vision includes thoughtfully hosted experiences in places Mandy knows deeply and values personally, including Fremantle, Bremer Bay, Shark Bay and Exmouth, along with selected destinations further afield.

The aim is simple: good people, well looked after, sharing time on the water in a way that feels calm, memorable and real.

About Mandy

Mandy is Aqualibrium’s founder, host and guide.

Qualifications and Experience:

  • Coxswain
  • Divemaster & Rescue Diver
  • Tour Guide, Kayak Guide, Fishing and Snorkelling Guide
  • Foundation-level Mountain Bike Instructor
  • Community Surf Rescue

She brings a rare combination of land and water capability, shaped by a life spent working and playing on, in and under the ocean. Her experience includes more than two decades of global guiding and marine work, as well as long periods living within local communities rather than simply passing through them.

Having called the coastlines of seven countries home, Mandy brings calm judgement, practical skill, curiosity and warmth to every experience she leads. She believes the best adventures come from staying longer, listening well, and helping people connect with nature, place and the moment they are in.

She is also multilingual, speaking fluent Spanish, along with conversational French and Indonesian, which helps many international guests feel welcome and at ease.

Her style is simple: arrive, be looked after, and enjoy something meaningful.

Not your typical retreat

Aqualibrium is not about forced activities or packaged group rituals. It is about laid-back adventure, time in nature, good company, and the ease of arriving knowing the details have already been thoughtfully handled.
